## 📋 What this app does

The software includes these features:
- SOS alerts: Send a signal to contacts with one click.

- Google Maps: View your surroundings on a clear map.
- Voice assistance: Use voice commands to initiate an alert.
- Multilingual support: Communicate in your preferred language.
- Responsive design: The screen adjusts to fit your window size.

## 🛠️ Using the application

The interface works intuitively. Keep these tips in mind as you learn the layout.

- Set your contacts first. Click the Settings gear inside the menu. Add phone numbers or email addresses of people you trust.
- Test the voice tool. Click the microphone icon to practice verbal commands. The app confirms your command with a sound.
- View the map. Click the Map button to center your current position. The icon updates as you move.
- Send an alert. Press the large red button on the home screen if you need assistance. The app sends your coordinates to your predefined contact list.

## 🛡️ Privacy and Safety

This software stores data locally on your computer. You choose which contacts receive your location. The application does not send your personal details to third-party servers without your permission. Check your browser settings to ensure the app has access to your location sensor. If the app cannot see your location, the map will not function. You can allow location access through the browser padlock icon in the address bar.

## 🧩 Common questions

Do I need to be online?
Yes, the application requires the internet to fetch map data and send emergency signals to your contacts.

Who receives my SOS alerts?
You choose your emergency contacts in the settings tab. Only these people receive your alerts.

Will it work in every country?
The map integration covers most regions. Voice assistance works in the languages enabled within your settings.

What if my internet connection fails?
The application works best with a stable link. If your connection drops, the app attempts to reconnect automatically to send your latest stored coordinates.

Can I change my settings at any time?
Yes, navigate to the menu at the top right of the screen. You can edit your contact list or change the language settings whenever you need.
